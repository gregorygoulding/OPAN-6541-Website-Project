Outside The City

Thanks to its convenient location, Budapest gives visitors access to several nearby areas with a wide range of things to do. From driving to another large city like Vienna, to exploring castles and wine regions, there are plenty of day trips you can take!

Day Trips from Budapest

Tip: take advantage of Hungary's easy-to-use, convenient train system to take day trips out of the city!

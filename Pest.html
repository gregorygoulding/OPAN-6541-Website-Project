Pest

The second half of the city, Pest, lies to the East of the Danube. Pest is the heart of downtown, and has tons of restaurants, hotels, and shops to explore. It's small enough to navigate on foot, but always has something new to explore!

See Pest on Google Maps

Tip: Make sure to pop into one of the city's many incredible cafes for a "Jeges Kavé" (iced coffee with iced cream)!
